Welcome to Crawford Guide Service, a premier Arizona Hunting Guide and Outfitter, where our goal is to make every hunt the hunt of a lifetime! As a resident of Arizona for more than 40 years, I grew up chasing Coues Deer, Mule Deer, Elk, Javelina, Turkey, and Quail. Hunting is my passion, and our goal at Crawford Guide Service is to give you the best hunting experience you’ve ever had. We are a professional big game hunting and upland game guide service with over 20 years of experience, providing fully guided archery, muzzleloader, rifle Deer, Elk, Javelina, Bear, Coyote and upland game hunting trips all over Arizona. We take pride in customizing each hunt to fit your particular needs. Whether you’re a junior hunter or a seasoned veteran, we are committed to giving you a hunting experience to rival all others.

Be sure to inquire about our over-the-counter archery Deer hunts that can be combined with Javelina archery hunts, as well as our fantastic over-the-counter junior Turkey hunts!
