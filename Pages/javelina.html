Javelina Hunting

Our guides at Crawford Guide Service have over 80 years of combined guided Javelina experience. We hunt on the Coronado National Forest, state land, and private ranches. A typical Javelina hunting day starts with an uphill climb on a steep and rocky mountain to our glassing spot. Our rifle hunts are glass-and-spot style. As your Javelina hunting guide, we locate the Javelina, and then stalk to within rifle range. Average rifle shots are around 100 yards. Our Javelina archery hunts are also spot-and-stalk.

Ask us about our over the counter guided deer and Javelina combo archery hunts for twice the hunting fun!

Hunting in Arizona can be physically demanding, and we encourage you to start an exercise routine so you can have a more enjoyable hunt.
