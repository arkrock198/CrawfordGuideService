Turkey Hunting

Let us be your next turkey hunting guide, and put our 40+ years of turkey hunting experience to work for you. Crawford Guide Service offers guided Merriam’s turkey hunts in units 1 and 27 on the Apache Sitgreaves National Forest in Northeast Arizona. We are also proud to offer fully guided Goulds turkey hunts right in our own backyard, in Tucson, a prime Goulds turkey habitat. A typical turkey hunting day begins before dawn. We walk long ridges calling for receptive toms. Once we have located a group of turkeys, we use decoys and calling to bring the birds into gun range. Let Crawford Guide Service take some of the work out of turkey hunting and deliver the fun!

Hunting in Arizona can be physically demanding, and we encourage you to start an exercise routine so you can have a more enjoyable hunt.
